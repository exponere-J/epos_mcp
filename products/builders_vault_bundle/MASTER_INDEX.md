## Reading paths by goal

### "I just want better prompts"
Read: CCP Pack → Voice Pack

### "I just want to market better"
Read: Brand DNA Kit → Signal Framework → Journey Map (in that order)

### "I just want to stop deploy failures"
Read: Pre-Mortem Kit (it's self-contained)

### "I'm rebooting my whole business"
Read: Brand DNA Kit → Journey Map → Signal Framework → CCP Pack → Voice Pack → Pre-Mortem Kit

(Takes ~8 hours spread across a week. Compounds daily.)

## Update policy

Each product evolves. Bundle owners get every major version update at
no additional cost. Check `products/<product>/README.md` for version
numbers. Newest updates land first on this repo's `main` branch.

## Single-team license note

"Single team" means up to 10 people at your organization. Over 10
people = Enterprise tier (separate SKU). Resellers / agencies = separate
SKU. Neither is priced here.
