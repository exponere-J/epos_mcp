## What this is

The "Dream 100" methodology combined with EPOS's FOTW (First Of Their
Watch) signal-extraction discipline. You stop making content from thin
air and start making content that answers what your market is already
saying — but too quietly for competitors to hear.

## Who this is for

Content creators, marketers, and founders who are tired of posting
into the void. The method assumes you already know your niche; it
gives you a disciplined way to listen to it.

## Who this is NOT for

Brand-new entrants who don't have a niche yet. The framework
presupposes you know which 100 voices matter in your space.
