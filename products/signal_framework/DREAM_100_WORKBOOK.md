## The audit (do after filling)

1. **Duplicate filter:** any voice appear in two categories? Keep in one.
2. **Pulse check:** have any of these been silent for 90+ days? Replace.
3. **Diversity check:** are 80% of voices from the same demographic /
   region / age cohort? Add 3 from outside it.
4. **Opposition check:** is there a voice on the list you *disagree*
   with but respect? If not, add one. You want intellectual friction.

## The setup (30 minutes)

1. Follow / subscribe / join all 100.
2. Create a dedicated tab/list in your feed reader of choice.
3. Schedule a daily 15-minute window to scan.
4. Schedule a weekly 60-minute window to synthesize via `SIGNAL_LOG_TEMPLATE.md`.

---

## Quarterly re-audit

Every 90 days, replace the bottom 20% of the list. Markets shift.
Your Dream 100 should shift with them.
