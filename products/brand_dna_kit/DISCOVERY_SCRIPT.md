# Brand DNA Discovery Script — 6 Buckets, ~24 Questions, 90 Minutes

> Run this with yourself or with a client. Record the conversation.
> Don't try to stay in-order. Follow the energy. Skip questions that
> don't land. 90 minutes is the cap — stop when you hit it.

---

## Bucket 1 — Origin (10 minutes)

1. Not "what do you do" — **why does this exist?** Tell me the
   specific moment you committed to building this.
2. What were you doing the day before you committed? What changed?
3. If you stopped today, who would be worse off? Name one person.
4. What's the thing you're convinced is true that most people in your
   space reject?

## Bucket 2 — Audience (15 minutes)

1. Think of the last three people who bought from you (or said they
   would). What do they have in common? **Not demographic.** Behavioral.
2. Describe their Monday morning between 8:30 and 9:00. Specifically.
3. What are they checking on their phone? What are they worried about?
4. What would they pay $100 to make stop happening?

## Bucket 3 — Objections (15 minutes)

1. Replay the last 5 "no"s you got. What exactly did they say?
2. Which of those do you think was the REAL reason, and which was
   the excuse?
3. What's the objection you wish you had a better answer to?
4. If you got a "yes" today, what would they have had to believe
   five minutes before saying it?

## Bucket 4 — Anti-avatar (10 minutes)

1. Who should NOT buy this? Describe them. Name their Monday morning.
2. What's the thing about them that makes the offering wrong?
3. What's the thing about your offering that they'd feel cheated by?
4. Write "this isn't for you if..." and finish the sentence three
   different ways.

## Bucket 5 — Value pillars (20 minutes)

1. What are the 3 things you do better than your competitors? Name
   them in 2 words each.
2. For each pillar: what's the concrete evidence? A case, a number,
   a specific example. Not "proven results."
3. Which pillar would you defend to the death? Which would you swap
   if asked?
4. If you had to drop 1 pillar and strengthen 1, which would go?

## Bucket 6 — Voice (20 minutes)

1. Find the last 3 pieces of content you wrote that felt like you.
   What do they have in common? (Highlight repeated words.)
2. What are 5 words you find yourself using that your competitors
   don't? (This is your vocabulary preference list.)
3. What are 5 words you deliberately avoid? (This is your avoidance
   list. It's as important as the preference list.)
4. If I read your content out loud, what would somebody guess about
   you? (Age, tone, register, background.) Is that what you want?

---

## After the 90 minutes

1. Transcribe the recording (or voice-memo summaries).
2. Fill out `TEMPLATE_BRAND_DNA_PROFILE.md`, quoting the speaker.
3. Delete everything generic. If it sounds like it could have come
   from any competitor, delete it.
4. Print the profile. Pin it. Use it.
