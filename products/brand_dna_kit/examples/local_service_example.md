# Brand DNA Profile — LuLu's House Cleaning (composite, illustrative)

*LuLu archetype — small local service business*

---

## Origin

LuLu started cleaning houses in 2019 when she lost her administrative
job and needed work she could do without an interview. Seven years
later she has a 3-person team and a reputation built entirely on
follow-through. She exists because the big cleaning franchises in her
metro area are "we'll be there between 10 and 2" companies; LuLu
shows up at 9:47 when she said 9:45.

## Audience — Susan, 62, in the house she's owned for 28 years

- **Role:** Retired school librarian, widow, lives alone
- **Monday 8:30am:** Waiting for someone (anyone) to do what they said
  they would. Anxious about scheduling because the last cleaner ghosted.
- **What she'd pay $100 for:** The cleaner to show up exactly when
  they said, and leave a note about anything they noticed (broken
  faucet, smell, etc.)
- **Content she reads:** Facebook local groups, Nextdoor, texts from
  her daughter

## Objections (verbatim)

1. "The last one took my key and never came back."
2. "Can I meet you first?"
3. "Can my daughter be here?"

## Anti-avatar

- This isn't for you if price is your top criterion. LuLu charges 20%
  more than the gig-app cleaners.
- This isn't for you if you want a faceless franchise. LuLu shows up
  personally for the first two visits.
- This isn't for you if you want an app to book. LuLu takes calls and
  texts only.

## Value pillars (3)

1. **Show-up rate** — 99.2% on-time in the last 200 appointments.
   Evidence: LuLu keeps a paper log.
2. **Same cleaner every time** — you have one person; you learn their
   name; they learn your house. Evidence: 87% of LuLu's clients have
   had the same cleaner for 2+ years.
3. **Written report after each visit** — paper note on the counter:
   "Noticed the guest room window is sticking. Also used the last of
   the Windex." Evidence: LuLu's clients show these notes to their
   kids as proof the cleaning happened.

## Voice

- **Prefer:** on time, the same Susan, paper note, your key
- **Avoid:** eco-friendly, streamlined, professional-grade, app,
  platform
- **Register:** a small-town service owner who answers her own phone
  and uses "ma'am." Warm, slow, reliable.
