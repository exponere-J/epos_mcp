# Brand DNA Profile — EPOS

*Completed: 2026-04-22* · *Re-run: 2026-07-22*

---

## Origin

EPOS exists because in 2025 I was losing my vision. I couldn't read a
screen for more than 15 minutes without a migraine. I was running a
pressure-washing business I couldn't see the invoices for, and trying
to learn to build software when most software assumes you can stare at
a terminal all day. I didn't want accommodations. I wanted an
**organism** that ran the business with me and read the screens when
I couldn't.

## Audience — Jamie Purdue (the one person)

- **Name:** Jamie (me) — and anyone like me
- **Role:** Solo founder / operator with a constraint that makes the
  standard playbook fail (vision, time, neurodivergence, location)
- **Monday 8:30am:** Checking the overnight CSV of invoices they
  couldn't manually process. Walking because sitting at a screen hurts.
- **What they'd pay $100 to make stop:** Having to read anything. Ever.
- **Content they read:** Indie Hackers (scanned), Lenny's (audio),
  occasional X on-topic threads, their own dictation transcripts

## Objections (verbatim)

1. "This sounds over-engineered for a one-person business."
2. "I don't have time to learn yet another framework."
3. "I need results this week, not philosophy."

## Anti-avatar

- This isn't for you if you want a faster horse. EPOS is a different
  animal.
- This isn't for you if you'd rather hire an agency than build a system.
- This isn't for you if "constitutional governance" sounds like fluff.

## Value pillars (3)

1. **Proprioception** — the system knows itself. Every component
   registered, every capability indexed, every decision audited.
   Evidence: Process Matrix Publisher refreshes 197-component state
   every 5 minutes.
2. **Discipline over novelty** — every build passes a governance gate
   before it ships. No stub code, no silent failures, no "it worked on
   my machine." Evidence: TTLG Anti-Truncation Contract; 6-check per
   file.
3. **Voice-first for the vision-impaired** — every screen-dependent
   operation has an audio alternative. Audio Triage is primary; visual
   is secondary. Evidence: Article XVI §3.

## Voice

- **Prefer:** organism, sovereign, discipline, constraint, register,
  compound
- **Avoid:** leverage, actually, game-changer, powerful, unlock,
  empower, transform, unleash
- **Register:** sounds like a vision-impaired founder talking into a
  walk-and-talk voice memo, transcribed by an agent that respects his
  phrasing. Plain-spoken, concrete, first-person.
