## Completion audit

After filling: count how many layers have "N/A" per touchpoint. If any
touchpoint has > 8 N/As, it's probably vestigial — consider removing it.
If TPs 14–31 have more N/As than TPs 1–13, your funnel is front-heavy
and retention/referral is where the opportunity is.
