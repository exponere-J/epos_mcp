# Consumer Journey Map — 31 Touchpoints × 13 Layers

**Version:** 1.0 · **Platform:** Gumroad · **Price test points:** $29 / $49 / $79

## What this is

The 31-touchpoint consumer journey map used to architect EPOS, plus a
13-layer depth template you apply to your own business. It's the
scaffold you wrap every piece of content, every email, every call
around — so nothing falls through the gap between "they heard of us"
and "they renewed."

## Contents

- `JOURNEY_MAP_GUIDE.md` — the methodology
- `TEMPLATE_31_TOUCHPOINTS.md` — the blank 31-touchpoint table to fill
- `LAYER_CHEATSHEET.md` — the 13 layers (identity, intent, data capture,
  classification, scoring, routing, delivery, feedback, learning,
  steward signal, measurement, continuity, reactivation)
- `BUILD_PDF.sh`

## Who this is for

Anyone whose funnel is "landing page → schedule a call" and nothing in
between. Anyone whose renewals fail because there's no mapped motion
between months 2 and 11.
