# Voice Pack — 30 Voice-Dictation Prompts

**Version:** 1.0 · **Platform:** Gumroad · **Price test points:** $19 / $29 / $49

## What this is

30 prompts optimized for voice dictation, plus a vocabulary-correction
dictionary that prevents common dictation errors from polluting your
transcripts. Built by someone who can't stare at screens — every prompt
assumes you're dictating while walking, cooking, or driving.

## Contents

- `VOICE_PACK_GUIDE.md` — why voice-first prompts are different
- `30_PROMPTS.md` — the full library, 6 categories × 5 prompts
- `VOCABULARY_CORRECTIONS.md` — 50 common dictation errors + corrections
- `BUILD_PDF.sh`

## Who this is for

- Vision-impaired founders / operators
- Walk-and-talk thinkers
- Anyone whose best ideas happen between 6 and 8 AM on foot
- People whose typing hands hurt by 3 PM

## Who this is NOT for

People who prefer typing and find dictation slow. The prompts are
optimized for speech patterns; they look awkward in a text editor.
