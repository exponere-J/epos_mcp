## AMENDMENT 1: THE PHILOSOPHICAL LAYER

### Phase I Gates (Manifesto Questions)
- **The Service Question**: Does this venture raise the level of service?
- **The Kaizen Question**: Can this venture improve 1% daily?
- **The Depth Question**: Does this venture go deeper than any competitor?
- **The 9th Order Question**: Does this venture deliver something the market has not imagined?

These are philosophical gates, not numerical scores. If the founder cannot articulate compelling answers, the idea stays in the Idea Log for maturation.
