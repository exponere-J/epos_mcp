# FOTW Expressions Map
## Six Sensors on the Same Nervous System
## For: CODE, Friday, TTLG, Content Lab

---

## ARCHITECTURE

Every expression feeds the same downstream: CCP disambiguates context, Intent Classifier tags, Format Decision Algorithm routes, Event Bus publishes, Context Vault stores, Sales Brain learns, Content Lab turns insights into content, TTLG calibrates diagnostics, Lead Scoring refines, Echolocation learns what resonates.

## EXPRESSION 1: Conversation Recorder
- **Captures**: Sales calls, Zoom, phone, in-person (with consent)
- **Output**: Timestamped transcript with speaker attribution
- **Triggers**: Sentiment tagging, intent classification, post-call AAR, Lead Scoring update, Sales Brain archive
- **Buyers**: Sales teams, consulting firms, customer service

## EXPRESSION 2: Chat Transmuter
- **Captures**: ChatGPT/Claude exports, Slack threads, email chains, Discord, iMessage
- **Output**: Normalized message chain, auto-generated deliverables (decks, docs, spreadsheets)
- **Triggers**: Format Decision Algorithm, Intent Graph, Gap Analysis, Enthusiasm Detection
- **Buyers**: Strategy consultants, product managers, startup founders

## EXPRESSION 3: Content Comment Listener
- **Captures**: LinkedIn, X, YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, Reddit comments
- **Output**: Engagement signal stream with sentiment, topic tags, intensity score
- **Triggers**: Echolocation scoring, Sequential Segmentation, serial poster detection, community routing, Lead Scoring enrichment
- **Buyers**: Marketing teams, community managers, brand strategists

## EXPRESSION 4: Browser Watcher (Passive Intelligence)
- **Captures**: Open browser tabs, articles, dashboards, competitor sites
- **Output**: Auto-indexed knowledge base from daily browsing
- **Triggers**: Research Engine enrichment, Competitive Intelligence, GRAG context, Market Signal Detection
- **Buyers**: Researchers, executives needing photographic memory of info consumption

## EXPRESSION 5: Meeting Synthesizer
- **Captures**: Full meeting recordings (audio + video + screen share)
- **Output**: Decision log, action items, follow-up sequences, deliverable generation
- **Triggers**: PM task creation, decision logging, follow-up email drafting, TTLG data feed
- **Buyers**: Teams that lose meeting output

## EXPRESSION 6: Real-Time Sales Guide (GRAG)
- **Captures**: Live interaction in progress (voice + screen + chat simultaneously)
- **Output**: Real-time talking points, objection handling, close detection
- **Triggers**: Sales Brain push, sentiment alerts, market-informed strikes, close detection
- **Buyers**: High-ticket sales teams. Premium add-on at +$149/month.
