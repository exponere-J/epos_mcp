## Mandate

The Sentinel surfaced three critical gaps that neither The Architect nor The Oracle caught:

1. **The Nebula creative buffer** — ideas had no staging area.
2. **The Audio Triage node** — the system waited for Jamie to read instead of proactively speaking.
3. **The Metabolic Governor** — the organism didn't protect its own architect.

These weren't feature requests — they were survival mechanisms. **The Sentinel sees what the organism needs to stay alive, not just what it needs to grow.**
